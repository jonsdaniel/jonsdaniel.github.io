Projects

Here are the projects I am working on. None of them are quite done yet, though.

Macronutrient Calculator

I've come across many people, including myself, who often decide they want to make a change in their health, but aren't quite sure where to start or what to do. I've spent over 6 years learning about health and fitness, and my biggest discovery is, it's not as hard as everyone makes it out to be. So, I decided to make an iOS app, that will make starting (and continuing) your health and fitness journey, easy, simple, and fun.

Application Tracker

When applying for software engineering jobs sometimes the applications can get very hard to keep track of, especially if you're applying well into the 100's. I had an idea to create a website that would allow you to track your applications, their status, and any other information about them in one responsive dashboard.

Car Payment Calculator

I'm someone who loves cars, and spends free time looking at cars I can't afford and wondering what I could do to afford them. So, I wrote my own iOS app to calculate a monthly car payment. This one is different because it allows you to modify a lot more variables than just vehicle price, downpayment, trade-in, sales tax, APR, and loan term.
